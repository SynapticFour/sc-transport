## Who needs a commercial license?

You need a commercial license if you:
- Incorporate sc-transport into a product or service that generates revenue
- Offer sc-transport as a hosted or managed service to third parties
- Use sc-transport as part of a commercial data transfer pipeline

You do **not** need a commercial license if you:
- Use sc-transport for research, evaluation, or education
- Publish academic papers using sc-transport results
- Contribute to sc-transport development

## What does a commercial license include?

- Right to use sc-transport in commercial products
- Priority support and SLA options
- Access to private roadmap and feature requests
- Custom integration support (optional)
